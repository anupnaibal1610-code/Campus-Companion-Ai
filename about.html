About the Project

About Campus Companion AI

An IBM University Engagement initiative designed to simplify student life through AI.

Our Mission

Simplifying Campus Life with Artificial Intelligence

Campus Companion AI was created as part of the IBM University Engagement program to demonstrate how enterprise AI can transform everyday student experiences.

Every day, students spend valuable time searching for basic information — exam dates, attendance policies, scholarship deadlines, hostel rules, and placement schedules. Campus Companion AI consolidates all of this into a single, intelligent, conversational assistant powered by IBM watsonx Orchestrate.

This website serves purely as the front-end interface. It does not build or train any AI model — instead, it provides a clean, modern, and responsive platform on which the watsonx Orchestrate agent can be embedded and made accessible to students.
